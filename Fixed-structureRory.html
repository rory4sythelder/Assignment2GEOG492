Cave Junction, OR:

Cave Junction is the only incorporated city and the largest population center in the Illinois Valley. It serves as a gateway to the nearby Oregon Caves National Monument, it's namesake. It is home to the famous Taylor's Sausage factory and restaurant. There are two high schools, Illinois Valley High and Kalmiopsis Community Arts Charter School, which was founded in 2023.

O'Brien, OR

O'Brien is a small unincorporated town located between Cave Junction and Crescent City. Commercially, the town has just a gas station, post office, one restaurant, and the Augustino Winery. It is known for being the hometown of Rory Forsythe-Elder.

Selma, OR

Selma is another unincorporated community within the Illinois Valley, located between Cave Junction and Grants Pass. Selma has a grocery store, post office, hardware store, and a bar. Lake Selmac in Selma is a popular recreation area.
